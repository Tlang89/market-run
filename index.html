THE IDEA
You’re running a lifetime hustle. Flip goods, move city to city, dodge heat, stay alive, and build power until you become The Pu$her.
FIRST MOVE
Look at the Black Market. Buy something you can afford. Don’t overthink it. Stay or travel, watch prices move, then sell when the price jumps.
THE MARKET
Buy low. Sell high. That’s the damn game.
🔥 HOT = price may climb.
📉 Falling / 💀 Crash = don’t be stupid.
Every city hits different.
STAY / TRAVEL
STAY = one day passes where you are.
TRAVEL = move cities and one day passes.
Every move burns time. This is a lifetime hustle.
CASH / BANK
Cash buys goods, pays hospital bills, and buys blocks.
Bank money is safer, but you gotta withdraw it before you spend it.
INVENTORY
Inventory shows what you carry and what it’s worth.
You got a limit, so don’t overload like an idiot.
STORAGE
Every city has its own stash spot.
STORE = hide goods in that city.
TAKE = grab them back later.
If you forget where you left your shit, that’s on you.
HEALTH / HOSPITAL
You can get hurt. Hospital fixes you, but it costs cash.
If health hits zero, your run is done.
HEAT / POLICE
Heat brings cops. Cops can fine you, chase you, bust you, or clean you out.
Move smart when heat gets ugly.
BLOCKS
$10,000 cash buys a block.
Own enough blocks and you become The Pu$her.
Bank money won’t buy a block until you withdraw it.
THE STREETS
Tonie = pressure.
Rico & Eddy = danger.
Cake Jaw = risky deal, maybe junk, maybe gem.
Perrkey = warning, but don’t trust her too much.
The Comedian = pain or gain. If you play a hand, choose The Gift, The Trade, or The Lesson.
SCRATCH TICKETS
Sometimes you find one on the street. Pick 1–5 and pray.
SAVE
The game auto-saves. You can also hit SAVE during a run.
Use CONTINUE SAVED RUN when you come back.
GAME OVER
Broke too long with no cash, no bank, and no inventory?
The Comedian ends your run.
RULE #1: Don’t get greedy. That’s how everybody loses.
